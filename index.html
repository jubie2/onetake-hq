Sales pitch
Never lead with AI. Lead with the problem. This script works for any local business.
Step 1 — Hook (cold call or DM opener)
"Hey, quick question — when a new patient calls your office after hours or on the weekend, what happens to that call?"
Step 2 — Agitate the problem
"Most [med spas / chiro offices / salons / restaurants] are losing 30–40% of new leads just because nobody answered. That's real money walking out the door every single day — and you'd never even know it."
Step 3 — Present the solution (still no AI talk)
"We set up a system that makes sure you never miss a lead again. It answers, qualifies, books appointments, and follows up automatically. Your team just shows up to confirmed bookings — no extra staff needed."
Step 4 — Social proof + urgency
"We're working with a few local businesses in San Diego right now and getting them 10–15 new leads per week they were previously losing. We're selective about who we bring on."
Step 5 — Close for demo (not the sale)
"Can I show you a 10-minute demo this week? No cost, no commitment — I just want to show you what it looks like running inside a business like yours."

Response: "That's great — this doesn't replace them. It handles after-hours, weekends, and the overflow when they're busy. It works alongside your team so nothing falls through the cracks."
Objection: "How much does it cost?"
Response: "It depends on your setup — but most clients are around $500–1000/mo. The average business we work with recovers that in their first 2–3 new clients. Want to see if it makes sense for you first?"
Objection: "Is this AI? I don't trust AI."
Response: "I get that. Think of it less like AI and more like an always-on receptionist — it's fully customized to sound like your business, and you review everything. You stay in control."
